c.thru.shell

[they/them]

The moniker of artist and data scientist Xristos K. Combining their creative and technical skills, Xristos creates audio and visual art. At a young age, they played guitar and made various forms of visual art, then went onto study Philosophy and Science. After undergrad, Xristos graduated from a data science bootcamp, where they learned the skills of a data analyst, scientist, and engineer.

They have written a series of data stories as a data journalist and researcher at Qri. As data and machine learning become more complex, and integrated with art, Xristos works as c.thru.shell, a project that utilizes their collective experiences and skills. This project isn't centered around any consistent style, it is an individual reflection of life in the 21st century with executive dysfunction.
